Here is a TOEIC Reading Comprehension (Part 7) question set:

---
**Article Type**: News Article
**Article Title**: New Investment Opportunities in Asia-Pacific Region
**Article Content**:
The Asian Development Bank (ADB) has announced new investment opportunities for businesses in the Asia-Pacific region. The bank's president, Takehiko Nakao, emphasized that these investments will help promote economic growth and job creation in developing countries. According to ADB statistics, the region has seen significant economic growth over the past decade, with many countries experiencing rapid urbanization and industrialization.

The new investment opportunities include funding for infrastructure development, renewable energy projects, and small and medium-sized enterprise (SME) financing. The bank will also provide technical assistance to governments and private sector companies to improve their capacity in areas such as policy reform, institutional strengthening, and project implementation.

**Questions and Options**:

Q1: What is the main purpose of the Asian Development Bank's new investment opportunities?
A. To promote economic growth and job creation in developing countries
B. To provide funding for large-scale infrastructure projects
C. To support small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) in the region
D. To improve policy reform and institutional strengthening

Q2: According to ADB statistics, what has been the trend in the Asia-Pacific region over the past decade?
A. Rapid decline of economic growth
B. Slow economic growth
C. Significant economic growth with rapid urbanization and industrialization
D. No significant changes in economic growth

Q3: What type of projects will the Asian Development Bank provide funding for, according to the article?
A. Only infrastructure development projects
B. Renewable energy projects only
C. Small and medium-sized enterprise (SME) financing and renewable energy projects
D. Infrastructure development, renewable energy projects, and small and medium-sized enterprise (SME) financing

**Answer Key**:
Q1: A
Q2: C
Q3: D
