Here are two TOEIC Part 6 Text Completion question sets:

**Set 1**

**Passage Title:** Job Description Change Notice

**Passage:**
As of next quarter, the Marketing Department will be undergoing some changes. Specifically, we will be adding a new team member to focus on social media campaigns. This change is necessary due to the growing importance of online marketing in our industry. As a result, (1) ____________________ will no longer be responsible for managing our company's Facebook page.

**Questions and Options:**

Q1. (for blank 1)
A. The current team member
B. A freelancer will be hired
C. We will outsource the task to an agency
D. The job will be eliminated

**Answer Key:** Q1: D
