Article Type: News Article
Article Title: New Office Building to Open in Downtown Area
Article Content:
The city's downtown area is about to get a new addition - a state-of-the-art office building. The $50 million project, scheduled to be completed by the end of this year, promises to bring in a fresh wave of business activity and jobs to the area.

Located on Main Street, the 10-story building will offer 20,000 square feet of office space, as well as amenities like a rooftop garden and fitness center. The building's design is inspired by modern architecture, with sleek lines and large windows that let in plenty of natural light.

The new office building is expected to attract a range of companies from various industries, including finance, technology, and healthcare. It will also include retail space on the first floor, which will feature local boutiques and restaurants.

"We're thrilled to bring this exciting project to life," said John Smith, CEO of the construction company behind the project. "We believe it will be a game-changer for the downtown area."

Questions and Options:
Q1: What is the estimated completion date of the new office building?
A. This year
B. Next year
C. End of this quarter
D. Early next month

Q2: What kind of amenities can you expect to find in the new office building?
A. Parking garage only
B. Rooftop garden and fitness center
C. Library and meeting rooms
D. Cafe and lounge area

Q3: Which industries are expected to be attracted to the new office building?
A. Only finance and technology
B. Finance, technology, and healthcare
C. Healthcare and tourism
D. Manufacturing and retail

Answer Key:
Q1: A
Q2: B
Q3: B
